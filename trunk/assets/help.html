Q: How do I launch Speaker Boost when I am in another application?
A: On Kindle Fire, you can tap on the left part of the status bar to pull down the notifications and then tap on "SpeakerBoost". In many apps the statusbar can be shown by tapping anywhere on the screen. On other devices, you can swipe down from the notification bar or hold the SEARCH button.

Q: How do I remove the on-screen ad?
A: Purchase any paid omega-Centauri application and the ad will disappear.

Q: Could I get a volume adjustment bar beside the boost bar?
A: Yes: just press the tools icon in Speaker Boost, tap on Settings, and there will be an option you can put a checkmark beside.
